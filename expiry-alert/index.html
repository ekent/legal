Privacy Policy for Expiry Alert

Last updated: December 24, 2024

Introduction

Expiry Alert ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Document names and types (e.g., "My Passport", "Driver's License")
  • Expiry dates
  • Optional notes
  • Optional document numbers (stored locally only)

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal identification information
  • We do NOT collect photos of your documents
  • We do NOT transmit any data to external servers
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your information, and it never leaves your device unless you choose to use iCloud sync (in which case, data is stored in your personal iCloud account).

Third-Party Services

Expiry Alert does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Notifications

We request permission to send local notifications solely to remind you of upcoming document expirations. These notifications are generated entirely on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
